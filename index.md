# About Me

<img src="https://caihanlin.com/caihanlin.jpg" class="floatpic">

Here is **Binglin Zhao** [(Linda, 赵冰琳)](https://www.eng.cam.ac.uk/profiles/hc663).<br>

I am a design researcher specializing in Human-Computer Interaction, Cognitive Load, and Human-Centered Design. My work focuses on understanding user behavior through empirical methods—particularly eye-tracking, behavioral experiments, and interactive system development—to create more intuitive and accessible interfaces.

I hold a Master’s in Design Science from Harbin Institute of Technology. My thesis explored cognitive load in surgical robot interfaces, combining quantitative analysis with interaction design to improve efficiency. I have hands-on experience conducting user research in clinical and educational settings, and I develop interactive experimental systems using Unity and Python to support accessible, real-world research.
