## 🎯 Research Vision
My overarching goal is to harness **artificial intelligence and optimization** to accelerate innovation in **low-carbon and sustainable energy systems**.  
I aim to bridge the gap between *materials, systems, and decision-making* through trustworthy, data-driven, and interpretable AI frameworks.

---

## 🔬 Research Interests

### Thermal Management and Energy Safety
  - Investigating mechanisms for suppressing thermal runaway in lithium-ion batteries and developing immersion cooling strategies
  - Thermal management of lithium-ion batteries under high-power discharge conditions
  - Conducting finite element modeling and thermal simulations  
  - Applications in electronic devices and energy storage systems  

### AI for Energy and Low-carbon Systems
- Data-driven modeling and optimization of energy systems  
- Machine-learning-based discovery and design of sustainable materials and devices  
- Carbon-footprint modeling, emission prediction, and low-carbon decision support  
- Digital-twin modeling and intelligent optimization for energy transition

### Scientific Machine Learning & Trustworthy AI
- Physics-informed, graph-based, and generative models for scientific data  
- Uncertainty quantification and interpretable AI for science  
- Trustworthy learning methods for energy and environmental applications
