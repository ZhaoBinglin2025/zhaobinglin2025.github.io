# 🚀 AI-Powered Customization Guide (with Antigravity)

This template is designed to be **AI-Native**. You don't need to be a CSS expert to have a stunning academic portfolio. By using an agentic AI assistant like **Antigravity**, you can evolve this site in minutes.

---

## 1. Quick Start: Use `_config.yml`
Most of your personal data is decoupled.
1. Open `_config.yml`.
2. Update the `owner` section with your name, university, and bio.
3. Your site will update automatically across all pages.

---

## 2. Using Antigravity (or other AI Coding Assistants)
To maintain the high-end look of this site, give specific, goal-oriented prompts to your AI assistant.

### Example Prompts for Antigravity:

#### **"Update my publication list"**
> *"I have a new paper titled '[Paper Title]' published in [Journal Name]. Please add it to my `publications.md` as a new card. Use an Emerald Green chip for the Impact Factor of [X.X] and add my advisor [Name] as a chip."*

#### **"Change the accent color"**
> *"I want to change the site's accent color from blue to a sophisticated 'Research Emerald' green. Please update the CSS variables in `main.css` and ensures it looks good in both Light and Dark modes."*

#### **"Add a new section"**
> *"Please add a 'Teaching' section to my homepage. Style it with the same minimalist card look used in the Publications page. I'll provide the course list in a moment."*

---

## 3. Why this works
Because this template uses **centralized CSS variables** and **Liquid templating**, the AI can understand the "Design System" and make changes that stay consistent with the theme.

**Enjoy your new AI-powered academic presence!** 🎓
